Mena 2 App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2025

📘 Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies solely to the Mena 2 mobile application. It does not apply to other services or products offered by TechSafe LLC.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information for resort operations and security:

  • Full name
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential address
  • Vehicle license plate number
  • QR codes scanned via the device camera

🛠️ How We Use Your Information

  • To identify and verify users on resort premises
  • To enable access to resort facilities via QR codes
  • To communicate account-related updates or resort notices

💡 Upcoming Features

A future feature will allow you to view your balance or amount due to the resort. This is for informational purposes only. The app does not collect, process, or store financial or payment data.

👤 Account Creation & Management

All accounts are created manually by resort administrators. Users cannot self-register through the app.

📷 Camera Usage

The app requests access to your device’s camera only to scan QR codes. It does not record, save, or transmit any photos or video.

🔐 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to third parties. Your data may only be disclosed if required by law enforcement or government authorities.

🧰 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

🗃️ Data Retention

Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or to meet legal obligations.

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data. All such requests must be directed to resort administration since accounts are created manually.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above and, where necessary, communicated within the app.
