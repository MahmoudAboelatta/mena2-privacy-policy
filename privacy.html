Mena 2 App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17 June 2025

This app is provided for the exclusive use of guests and staff at Mena 2 Resort in Egypt. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly.

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal data to provide services and maintain security at the resort:

  • Full name
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential address
  • Vehicle License plate number
  • QR codes scanned via device camera

How We Use Your Information

  • To identify and verify users at the resort premises
  • To allow access to resort facilities through QR code validation
  • To communicate with you if needed (e.g. account-related updates)

Upcoming Feature Disclosure

A future feature will allow you to view your current balance or amount due to the resort. This is for informational display only. The app does not collect or process any financial or payment information.

Account Creation and Management

Account creation is managed solely by resort administrators. Users cannot self-register in the app.

Camera Usage

The app requires access to your device’s camera only to scan QR codes. It does not take, record, or store any photos or videos.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your information may only be disclosed if legally required (e.g. by law enforcement authorities).

Security

We implement appropriate technical and administrative safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Your Rights

You may contact us to review, update, or request deletion of your data. Since accounts are created manually by resort staff, requests must go through the official resort contact.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date above and notify users through the app if necessary.
